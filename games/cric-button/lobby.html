About CricSwing

This is an independent cricket-inspired game. It is not affiliated with or endorsed by any cricket board, tournament, team, player, league, or governing body. All teams, leagues, and player names are fictional or user-customized.

Historical chase scenarios are presented as creative inspiration only and are compressed into final-over challenges. The originals are not recreated faithfully.

How to Play
  1. Pick a mode, country/city, format, and difficulty on this phone.
  2. Hold the phone like a bat. When the ball is bowled, swing to time your shot.
  3. Chase down the target before you run out of balls or wickets.
